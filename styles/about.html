RADIO & MIC

Tune in,Stand out

Welcome to RADIO & MIC, where every voice finds its rhythm! We are more than just a radio station—we are a platform for creativity, connection, and expression. From live shows and student-curated playlists to insightful discussions and campus news, we bring the pulse of student life straight to your ears. Whether you want to share your thoughts, discover new music, or stay updated on the latest happenings, RADIO & MIC is your go-to destination. Tune in, get involved, and let your voice be heard!

RADIO & MIC is more than a station—it’s the heartbeat of campus life, amplifying student voices, sparking conversations, and bringing entertainment, news, and music straight to you.
